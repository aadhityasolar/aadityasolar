Our Story

A Team Built on 15+ Years of Experience

Aadhitya Solar was founded by Deepak and is led together with Co-Founder Gnanaprakash. Our two-member founding team brings deep, hands-on experience in the solar energy industry — from system design and installation to net metering and government subsidy processing.

Over the years, our team has delivered reliable, cost-effective solar solutions for homes, farms and businesses across Tamilnadu, completing 500+ installations in 6 districts. Every project is personally planned, supervised and quality-checked by our founders.

We are committed to making clean energy accessible to every household and enterprise — helping you save on electricity bills while contributing to a greener planet.
